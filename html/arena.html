Arena Information

Facility

Outdoor arena with packed sand base, lighting for evening events, and spectator seating. Limited covered staging area and portable restroom on site.

Rules & Safety

  • All riders must wear appropriate footwear and helmets for youth classes.
  • Clean up after your horse — manure must be removed from staging areas.
  • No unauthorized motorized vehicles on the arena surface.
  • Pets must be leashed.

Rental & Fee Guide

Hourly Arena Rental: $30/hr (non-members)
Event Day Rental: $200/day + cleaning deposit
School / Group Rates: Contact for quote.
